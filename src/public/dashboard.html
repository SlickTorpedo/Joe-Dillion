Progressive Training Tips

Start Light: Begin with just 1 pound and focus on proper form rather than heavy weight.
Consistency is Key: Progress slowly but consistently. Adding weight too fast can lead to injury.
Listen to Your Body: If a weight feels too challenging, stay at your current level for another week.
Perfect Your Form: Higher repetitions help build muscle endurance and perfect your technique.
